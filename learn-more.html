About TennAi

Every business, no matter how big or small, relies on customer feedback for retention and improving products. TennAi is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize how businesses collect, analyze, and respond to customer feedback. The unique combination of features coupled with the incredible benefits it provides make it an essential for businesses aiming to enhance customer satisfaction and increase their audience. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and natural language processing, TennAi empowers businesses to improve customer relations, identify market trends, and make data-driven decisions. TennAi includes features such as a survey builder, real-time sentiment analysis, an insight dashboard, smart responses, and predictive analytics. With the customizable survey builder, TennAi creates dynamic surveys tailored to specific business needs and increased participation rate. The sentiment analysis tool analyzes customer feedback and categorizes responses by theme such as satisfaction or service quality. This information can be easily viewed in the automated insight dashboard, where data is visualized in a way that highlights key trends, customer pain points, and recommendations for taking action. Customers are also incentivized to participate in giving feedback by having simplified buttons and a customizable reward system for partaking in the survey. A system within TennAi also flags potential spam and abuse of the reward system, preventing unwanted feedback and loss of resources. TennAi is also capable of identifying potential issues and future opportunities through predictive models. TennAi brings priceless information by transforming customer feedback into actionable insights and enables businesses to drive customer satisfaction and loyalty while uncovering hidden opportunities for growth.
